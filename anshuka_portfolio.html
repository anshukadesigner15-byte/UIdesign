About Me

Designing interfaces people love to use

I'm a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer with 9 years of hands-on experience in crafting visually compelling, user-friendly, and fully responsive digital products. Currently making an impact at Contrado Imaging India.

My design philosophy centres on the intersection of aesthetics and function — every pixel should serve a purpose. I translate business goals into digital experiences that feel natural and effortless for users.

Beyond design, I bring solid frontend development skills in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, allowing me to bridge the gap between design vision and engineering implementation.
